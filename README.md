* Law No. 6563 on the Regulation of Electronic Commerce and related legislation,
* Turkish Penal Code No. 5237,
* Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (“KVKK”)

Use of Cookies
Appointment may collect, process and securely store your browsing information within the framework of its legal obligations and provided that it is not used outside the purposes determined in this policy. Cookies are small text files saved by your browser when you visit the Appointment website or install the mobile application on your device. These cookies are used to show you personalized advertisements, offer promotions, personalize content, and improve application performance.
Randevu can improve the user experience by matching data collected at different times. These processes are only carried out within the scope of this Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy.
Cookie Types:
* Session cookies expire when the browser is closed.
* Persistent cookies remain on your device for a certain period of time.
You can change your cookie preferences or disable them completely from your browser settings. However, in this case, some features of Randevu may work in a limited way.
Randevu may also process anonymous user data for statistical purposes using Google Analytics or similar analytical tools. You can review Google's data collection policy at Google Privacy Policy.

Sharing Personal Data with Official Authorities
Randevu may share user data only with public institutions and organizations legally authorized to request it, in cases required by legislation. This sharing is only done in accordance with the official requests of the relevant institutions and within legal limits.

Security of Personal Data
LiMobile Technology takes all necessary technical and administrative measures to prevent unauthorized access to personal data. All data is kept confidential and shared with third parties only under legal obligations or with explicit consent. In the event of any data breach, users and the Personal Data Protection Board (KVKK) will be immediately informed.
